How the definition was earned — the changes are themselves data

Three schemes once coexisted; two were killed by the lock. Then testing sharpened two boundaries. Every change carries a decision + an IRR receipt — the instrument's revision history is part of its defense.

So "what happened to T5": scholarly commentary moved down from the skeleton's T8 to canonical T5; "compulsory belief" folded into T6. The drift was caught as a semantic divergence wearing identical labels — which is exactly why the lock had to happen before thread-assembly.
2 · T8/T9: provisional → locked (IRR97, same session, 5/5). All five cohorts independently said lock — none said fold them away. The five refinements that came back are now part of the instrument: (1) T8 must name what it recovers (recovers_target_t1 + target_t6t7 + strategy); (2) T8→T9 transition ≈ recovery-rhetoric fades into normalized transmission, ~2 generations; (3) the CARE carve-out above; (4) T8-seed vs T8-active — the recovery instrument (Philokalia) vs the movement (Hesychast revival); (5) T8 and T9 co-occur at different scales — a single sermon can be a T8-move inside a T9-lineage.
Refinements #4 and #5 were born from disagreements: Haiku's solo dissent on the Philokalia (4/5) and a 3/5 scale-split on the Womanist reading. Consensus was not forced — the dissents became structure.
3 · Lock v2 (Decision 76): testing exposed two soft boundaries. The v2 prompt had invented an unratified "academic→T10" route, splitting the T5 vote (78%, misroutes landing in T10). The repair: T5 carries an emic/etic stance (both are still T5; the register test governs, not private belief), and T10 requires an artistic medium — outside-the-lineage alone is not enough; prose scholarship is never T10.

The actual prompt — what the LLM coder is told, verbatim

From pm/30_methodology/pass2_t_stage_prompt_v3_2026-05-31.md (the live instrument). Three things to notice: assignment is seed-relative (the same passage can sit at different stages for different seeds); the easy cases never reach the LLM (deterministic pre-fill); and the hard boundaries get explicit discriminator tests, in order.
THE CORE INSTRUCTION + THE SCHEME
Place the PASSAGE in the transmission lineage of the SEED. Canonical scheme (lineage T1–T9; T10 orthogonal):
T1 originating insight · T2 direct-witness/first encoding · T3 textual codification · T4 institutional
canonization · T5 scholarly commentary · T6 confessional/sub-tradition codification · T7 degraded popular
form WITHIN the lineage · T8 revival/recovery movement · T9 living lineage · T10 (orthogonal) artistic
reimagination outside the lineage.
THE T5 / T6 DISCRIMINATOR — argues vs locks
T5 = scholarly commentary that ARGUES doctrine through exegesis + systematic argument + polemical defense
(Bunyan; Wesley's sermons; Aquinas's Summa; anti-Calvinist polemic). T6 = confessional boundary-drawing
documents that DEFINE doctrine as institutionally binding (Westminster Confession; Trent canons).
Discriminator: if the text ARGUES doctrine (even passionately + at length), it's T5. If the text LOCKS
doctrine as institutional canon (binding-for-the-tradition; subscription-required), it's T6.
THE EMIC / ETIC REGISTER TEST (v3)
Within T5, set t5_stance. T5-emic = confessional commentary arguing FROM INSIDE the tradition's frame.
T5-etic = critical/secular/academic study analyzing the tradition AS OBJECT-OF-STUDY.
Discriminator — the WHO-IS-SPEAKING-AS test: is the author writing AS a professing member arguing the
doctrine (emic), or AS a scholar/critic studying it (etic)? Register governs, not private belief —
a believing scholar writing in academic register = etic. Both stances are still T5.
T8 DETECTION + THE SCALE RULE
A text or movement whose FUNCTION is to diagnose a T6/T7 calcification and recover a specific T1 is T8 —
even if its MATERIAL is older (a compilation of T5 patristic texts that re-ignites a dead practice is T8,
t8_phase=seed, material_t_stage=T5). If T8, you MUST fill recovers_target_t1 + recovers_target_t6t7 +
recovery_strategy.
Scale rule: a SINGLE contemporary reading performing a recovery move within a living tradition is T9 with
t_stage_secondary_function="T8-function" — reserve T8-primary for the organized movement or its seed-text.
THE T9 / T5-ETIC / T10 THREE-WAY (v3 — discriminators in order)
(a) Does the speaker identify AS a current practitioner speaking-as-such? → T9
(b) Is it scholarly/critical study in PROSE? → T5, stance=etic
(c) Is it outside the lineage AND in an artistic medium (in art's own grammar)? → T10
"Outside the lineage" alone is NOT enough for T10 — without an artistic medium it is T5-etic.
THE CARE CARVE-OUT (verbatim, in the prompt itself)
If the tradition is Indigenous/oral/continuous-transmission with no T6/T7 calcification, it may be T9 with
t1_t9_collapse=true (no T8). If it recovers from EXTERNAL disruption (colonialism, boarding schools, archive
capture, language loss), set reclamation_under_disruption=true and do NOT tag T8.
What the coder returns — strict JSON, one row per (passage × seed): the lineage position t_stage T1–T9 · t5_stance · the T8 relational fields · separately, optional t10_membership (+ severity + medium) · a one-sentence rationale · a 0–1 confidence · assignment_method (deterministic-prefill vs LLM). A source can carry layered tags at once — Romans: originating=T1 · text=T2 · canonical_collection=T4 — Pass-2 emits the layers, never a single scalar.

How an assignment becomes trusted — the reconciliation chain

No single model's answer is the answer. The chain, in order:
1 · Deterministic pre-fill. The seed's own text is never "decided" — it inherits the seed's layered tags mechanically. Only genuinely interpretive cases reach a model.

2 · The panel votes. Five cohorts proofread the assembler's assignments per passage (the IRR101 pattern); votes bucket as UNANIMOUS / STRONG-MAJORITY / MAJORITY / SOLO. Final re-fire on the Romans 59: 41% unanimous, 34% strong-majority, SOLO down to 2%; assembler↔majority 85%.

3 · Disagreement is kept, and sometimes promoted. Haiku's solo Philokalia dissent didn't get outvoted into silence — it became refinement #4 (T8-seed vs T8-active). The Womanist-reading scale-split became refinement #5. The instrument grows at exactly the points where readers diverge.

4 · The cheap-coder gate (W8 — graduated). Before production scaled, the validation gate proved the cheap model matches the expensive 3-model consensus 100% on T-staging — so production coding runs on the validated single coder, with periodic sampling checks. Reliability is then graded per cell (passage × dimension, the R-grade matrix): the same passage can be highly reliable on T-stage and weak on another axis, and the matrix says so.

5 · §2.1 stands over all of it. Every coded value is a CANDIDATE under review, not a finding. The finding is what survives aggregation, re-fires, and the researcher's walk.
