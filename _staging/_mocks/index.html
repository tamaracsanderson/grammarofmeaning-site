Method test-bed — replications ★ entry 01

Entry 01 — animating a still picture's repeating elements (Willett et al., UIST 2018) tried on a ca.1600 Persian miniature and a Jakuchū woodblock. Five bays per test: paper · input · interaction · output · post-mortem. Two of three runs are partial or failed by design, with recall stated (~10 of ~25 objects) — the negative results are the finding.
