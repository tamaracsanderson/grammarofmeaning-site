C — Matisse cut-out leavespapiers découpés · figure-ground

A bold, hand-cut organic frond — asymmetric, lobed, alive — on a cream ground that is co-equal negative space. Cut-paper is figure-ground, which is the house's negative-space method. These flat shapes hold hardest at 32px.

Favicon test — C1 across chromelight vs dark tab

C1 (the Matisse cut leaf) is the master candidate — a single bold cut-paper frond that holds hardest at 32px. On light chrome the frond is moss on cream; the dark-chrome variant inverts figure-ground so the mark keeps its contrast — the frond lightens to sage on a deep field.
