The chosen direction — in green & gold

Deep-green ground · a light-beige card with a double gold border · a larger Romans title · the house sprig as the interstitial. Two grounds to compare: a plain green, and one with a public-domain botanical stamped on.

On the image treatment: for a reading surface I went with one large botanical stamped on the green — cream, monotone, like a foil-stamp on a book cover — rather than a tiled quilt (busy + seams) or stained-glass/tiles (too fragmented to read against). The stamp here is a real Anna Atkins fern (1843, public domain), desaturated to cream and screen-blended so only the fern prints on the green. Quilt / tiles stay open as a denser "maximal" option if you want to see them.
