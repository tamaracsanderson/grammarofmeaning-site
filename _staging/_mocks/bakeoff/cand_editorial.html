How meaning gets made.

A bottom-up study of the moves that recur — as a rhyme, not a copy — across ~545 traditions read in their own vocabulary. Meaning is made in the in-between.

01Direction · Mood

Field Notes — a research journal that reads like the best science magazine.

Not a craft object. A precise, contemporary publication: Swiss grid, generous whitespace, a single confident accent, and every diagram built to Tufte's rule — maximum data-ink, honest labels, high contrast. The palette is warm-white and structural, so the accent does one job: signal. Restraint is the identity.

Inter carries every label, nav item, and diagram annotation at high legibility — a neutral, exacting workhorse. IBM Plex Mono handles data, citations, and figure numbers, giving the research an instrument-panel precision.

Pairing rationale. A warm literary serif for meaning (Newsreader) against an exacting neo-grotesk for structure (Inter), arbitrated by a mono for data (Plex Mono). The serif says human; the grotesk says serious contemporary publication; the mono says this is measured, not asserted. Three registers, one clear hierarchy.

04Grid · Rules · Motif

System, not decoration. A 12-column Swiss grid; an 8px baseline; hairline rules (#DEDACE) that only ever separate, never ornament. Whitespace is the primary material. The single motif is the rotated square node — a "move" as a discrete unit — echoed in the brandmark, the diagram nodes, and the expand marker. CSS-only; no images.

D · "For the curious" — designed, not an afterthought
+ Why "rhyme," and not "the same thing"? FOR THE CURIOUS · 2 min

Two poems rhyme when their endings agree, even though the words differ. A cross-tradition rhyme is the same: two moves share a shape — agent, substrate, operation, outcome — while the vocabulary, the metaphysics, and the world behind them stay entirely their own.

Not equivalence
We never flatten one tradition into another. The Big-T claims stay tradition-internal.
Not influence
A rhyme needs no contact. Two voices can land on one shape independently.
Portable, little-t
The recurring move travels. That portability is what the study is after.
Earned, not assumed
A candidate rhyme stays "unranked" until the corpus supports it.
