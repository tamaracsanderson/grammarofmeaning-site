Now it's actually glass

The three things it was missing: a leaded glass field (the grisaille diamond quarry, with dark came-lines), real foiled tracery (a quatrefoil rose, not three dots), and jewel color across the whole window. Harmonized to the house — moss, gold, ruby, amber, with a single cobalt nod — and kept warm and luminous like a design-cartoon, not Chartres-dark, so the gold still pops.

How the re-weight works now (native to glass): forward = a medallion in colored glass (as here); receded = the same medallion in grisaille / clear glass (like the sealed niche) — so a Forensic-1840 window would keep Judgment + Atonement in jewel color while Liberation, Union, Communion drop to clear quarry glass, and a 2020 window reverses it. That's how a real window shows emphasis — colored vs. clear — instead of fading on paper.
