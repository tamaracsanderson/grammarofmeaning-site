the living altar · direction 1 · the re-weight

Same grammar, re-weighted

One window, three settings. Every panel holds the same seven aspects in the same slots. What changes is which aspects glow (carry the weight), which recede to line (present, transformed), and which are gone — an empty pane open to the sky. That is the claim: a tradition's soteriology isn't one fixed doctrine, it's a re-weightable set of moves. Panels A and B move through time; panel C proves the grace↔effort dial is independent of what's foregrounded.

A · c. 1840

Judgment and Atonement carry the weight; grace does the work. Liberation is an empty pane — not yet thinkable.

B · c. 2020

Liberation and Belonging carry the weight; participation does the work. Atonement and Union recede (transformed); Judgment is open to the sky — Hell erased.

C · off-diagonal proof

Same foreground as 2020 — but the dial at grace (Liberation as God's act: the Exodus, the Harrowing). Nothing changes but the dial — so the dial is independent of the operations.

● full + halo = carries the weight  ·  ○ pale line = present but receded (transformed)  ·  ☁ open sky = absent (erased — the pane isn't there)  ·  the sealed niche holds in all three

Read the flip (A → B): Judgment + Atonement glow in 1840 and, by 2020, Atonement recedes to line while Judgment is gone (open sky); Liberation crosses from absent (an empty 1840 pane) to foreground; Belonging and the effort-dial rise. Read the proof (B → C): the operations are identical — only the dial moves, from effort to grace. If foregrounding Liberation forced effort, C would be impossible; it isn't. The agency dial is a second, independent axis. Nothing is ever added or cut from the grammar — only the weighting moves.
