Same grammar, re-weighted

One window, shown twice. Every panel holds the same seven aspects in the same slots — nothing is added or removed. What changes is which aspects glow and which recede. That is the whole claim: a tradition's soteriology isn't one fixed doctrine, it's a re-weightable set of moves, and different moments dial the weights differently.

A · c. 1840

Judgment and Atonement carry the weight; grace does the work.

B · c. 2020

Liberation, Communion and Union carry the weight; participation does the work.

● full + halo = the aspect carries the weight  ·  ○ pale line = present but receded (drawn, not glazed)  ·  the sealed niche holds in both

Read the flip: the top of the window (Judgment) and the atonement medallion glow in 1840 and thin to line in 2020; Liberation + Communion + Union do the reverse. The Grace ↔ Effort dial slides from grace (1840) to effort (2020). Nothing is added or cut — the grammar is constant; only the emphasis moves. That is what makes it one tradition re-weighted rather than two.
