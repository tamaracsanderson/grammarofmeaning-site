Howard Thurman

1899 – 1981

Mystic-theologian · Dean of Marsh Chapel, Boston University · co-founder, the Church for the Fellowship of All Peoples

A mystic of the Black church who held the inner life and the work of liberation as one thing. His Jesus and the Disinherited (1949) became a touchstone of the civil-rights movement. He preached the "sound of the genuine": the true self that waits and listens beneath the noise.

"There is a sound of the genuine in you."

— The Sound of the Genuine, Spelman College, 1980 · verified
