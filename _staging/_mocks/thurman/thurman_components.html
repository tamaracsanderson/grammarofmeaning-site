The voice archive · design system

One engine, many voices

Every voice in the archive is the same machine, reconfigured. Change four dials and fill the labels — the rest is shared. This is the kit.

01 What you change per voice

The four dials

A voice is a reconfiguration, not a redesign. Turn four dials — source, palette, pixel, song — and every screen, label, and interstitial inherits the change. Nothing else is touched.

Dial 01

Source

The real footage / imagery register that gets pixelized live in the browser.

Thurman → ocean / deep water
Plum Village → still river · rain · drifting cloud

a life in the archive

Howard Thurman

1899 – 1981

Mystic-theologian · Dean of Marsh Chapel, Boston University · co-founder, the Church for the Fellowship of All Peoples

A mystic of the Black church who held the inner life and the work of liberation as one thing. He preached the "sound of the genuine": the true self that waits and listens beneath the noise.

Courtesy of the Howard Gotlieb Archival Research Center & the Howard Thurman Center for Common Ground, Boston University. Quotes verified per §13. Schematic mock · Stage 6 = 0.
