Voice garden · Howard Thurman · artist-persona bakeoff

Seven hands, one gesture

Not five themes by one designer (that was the PowerPoint) — seven artists, each committed to a real medium. The divergence is the hand, not the theme. Open each full-screen and draw a voice.

The shared spine (identical in all seven): not a roulette — drawing a voice, at random, from an archive of hearts. The wave is the many (the archive), the pulse is the one (the voice you drew); landing is wave→pulse. A spiritual — Deep River — beneath his voice.

The point is to see seven real artistic takes, not seven tasteful variants. Pick one to develop (or a hybrid). Then the design-crit panel scores them.
