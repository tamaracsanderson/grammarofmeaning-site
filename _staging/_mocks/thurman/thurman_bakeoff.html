The Thurman sermon roulette — five skins

Same mechanic in every one — spin, decelerate, land on a real Thurman sermon. Five genuinely different feels, each grounded in one of his works. Spin each; then pick a direction (or a hybrid). Dark-luminous · oceanic · botanical · sonic · still.

Skin 1The Luminous Darkness

His 1965 book — light living inside the dark; points that drift and coalesce, resolving to stillness on land.

Skin 2Deep River

His meditation on the spirituals + his ocean childhood — generative waves; the sermon rises from the deep.

Skin 3The Growing Edge

The oak of his Daytona childhood — the spin grows and branches; a landed sermon blooms.

Skin 4The Sound of the Genuine

His 1980 Spelman address — a luminous waveform; the visual is the sound (binds to the real recording later).

Skin 5Meditations of the Heart

His book of meditations — the quietest. A heartbeat made visible on still water: ripples pulsing to a lub-dub, the contrast direction, for silence.

On this bakeoff Five divergent directions on purpose — the point is to see them side by side, not to converge early.
