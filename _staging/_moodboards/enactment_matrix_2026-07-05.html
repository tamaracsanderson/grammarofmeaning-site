Enactment Practices — two matrices 🌿

How a text is lived out, made systematic. Matrix A = each tradition × the scale-of-concern rings (S1→S6-8) — so you can see a tradition's whole range and where it's thin. Matrix B = each enactment category × tradition — so you can see the cross-tradition families (all the giving practices side by side, etc.).
★ Reading the gaps honestly (the cherry-picking check). The enactment DR is exemplar-based (a practice or two per cell), so hatched ▧ cells are gaps — some real (a tradition genuinely emphasises other rings) and some just research-thin (we haven't sourced it yet). Per the DR: the Abrahamic traditions are historically thin on S5 (living earth); the Indigenous traditions bridge S5↔S6-8; Jainism's S5 is driven by an S1 goal (soul-purification). Rings: S1 interior · S2 near-other · S3 community · S4 society · S5 living-earth · S6-8 cosmos/divine.
