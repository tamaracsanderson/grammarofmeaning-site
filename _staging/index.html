Grammar of Meaning

How meaning gets made.

Meaning isn't found — it's made, in the meeting between a person and the world.

Grammar of Meaning is an open, bottom-up study of how meaning gets made — the category that religion, philosophy, and psychology all share. It reads each tradition in its own vocabulary to ask which moves underneath the answers recur as a rhyme across traditions.
