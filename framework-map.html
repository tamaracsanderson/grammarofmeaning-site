Where everything sits

The established framework — 7 layers. Every piece of work goes into one of these. Nothing is a new thing until it's placed here and can't fit. Refreshed 2026-07-15 — see the revision log at the foot.

The rule (place-before-spawn). Before introducing anything — a term, a category, a viz, a move — ask: which of the 7 layers is this? If it fits an existing layer, it goes there (that's ~99% of the time). If it genuinely fits nowhere, that's the rare signal it may be a new layer — and then it's a deliberate CLC/IRR extension, never an ad-hoc spawn. This is the antidote to the S147–S149 drift (inventing nature/disposition instead of using the real apparatus).

The Coordinate — the 6 fields

Where does a framing sit? (its position)
E·O·M·T·S·ground — every figure/framing gets a 6-tuple (one value per field, from the fixed menus). This is the atom of the method.
What lives here: the 6 axes + their value menus · multi-label (a figure can hold ≥1 value on a field) · fit-or-extend (menus controlled + extensible via IRR) · the MECE / misfit check.
⚠ CHANGED 2026-07-15 (IRR195, 5/5): the weight is out. Asking a coder for 60/40 vs 55/45 is precision the judgment can't support (our own anchoring bug proved it). Use a calibrated coarse ordinal — Single · Lean · Dual — and derive the fuzzy centroids downstream; the coder never emits a number. And estimability is axis-dependent: E + M are prevalence-estimable; O + ground-world-relation are architectonic (an apophatic qualifier governs every predicate — "what percent" is a category error) → set/dominance only. Open: is our sum-to-100 vector a mixture or graded set-membership? They're different semantics.
⚠ CORPUS LAYERS (2026-07-15) — "not-coded ≠ not-held": 1717 live voices, 536 coded (31%). So a coordinate can look empty because the figure is present but uncoded. Layers: L1 coded (536 / 365 distinct tuples) · L2 metadata-only (~1181, we own them) · L3 secondary-source-reachable (the Merleau-Ponty case) · L4 true gap. This distinction governs Layer 7.

3

The 2×2 — the negative-space finder

What's missing? (the gaps)
visibility × salience — the opaque×in-focus cell = the gap. Plus the seer (opaque to whom: agent / narrator / reader).
What lives here: the 2×2 · the 3 seers · FILL / SUBSTITUTE / BARRED — and the frame-jump: within one seer's viewpoint you can only FILL; a SUBSTITUTE voids the premise and is the door out to an adjacent frame (a different seer/Sitz) · the flavors of silence.
Resolutions ARE edges: filled-internally (a completion edge — and it can run backward: a later move filling an earlier gap is suspense) → generatable → RESIDUAL (a reception edge, pointing out of the text, where readers fork). The residuals are where the cross-tradition question lives.

5

The Bidirectional Trace-Tree

Where did it come from / where did it go? (provenance + reception)
Around a pivot: backward = provenance-regression; forward = reception. Run independently, join at the pivot. Edges tagged textual / attested / reconstructed.
What lives here: the two trees (golden chain + She Loves You) · Saquib's tree · the interactive version (design-SB).
★ NEW 2026-07-15 — the counterfactual trace-tree. Substitute one slot → the edge fans into admissible next-moves. Two views of one object: the fan (split at every fork — the space, ~3depth; undrawable past ~5 levels but perfectly holdable as a dataset) vs a world-line (one coherent path — a single alternate song).
The TWO GATES (IRR196 confirmed the decomposition): era-availability × path-coherence. A move can be era-OK yet world-incoherent (it belongs to a different world-line). Required change: path-coherence must become a computed coordinate-state constraint (≤1-step shift on the layer-1 axes), not a holistic judgment — the #1 risk, and the next build.
Convergence = forward-fan ∩ backward-funnel — not forward=backward. They're different objects: the ending is a wide funnel backward, a narrow target forward. Reporting only the intersection is the anti-teleology rule made mechanical.
